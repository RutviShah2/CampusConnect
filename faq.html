❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! 💬 Here’s a quick guide to help you navigate CampusConnect with ease. If you're ever stuck, just know you're not alone — we're here for you! 💙

  • Q: How do I join a club?
    A: Go to the Clubs page and explore different clubs based on your interests. Each listing has info on how to join or register! 🎭✨
  • Q: Where can I find upcoming events?
    A: Visit the Events page to stay updated on festivals, competitions, orientations, and more! 📅🎉
  • Q: Can I register online?
    A: Absolutely! Head over to the Registration page and fill in your details. It’s fast, easy, and mobile-friendly. 📝📲
  • Q: What if I miss an announcement?
    A: Don’t worry! All announcements are saved on the Announcements page — check there anytime to catch up. 📢
  • Q: Is there a way to view past memories or event photos?
    A: Yes! Visit the Memories section and relive your favorite campus moments. 📸💖
  • Q: Who built CampusConnect?
    A: It was lovingly created by Rutvi Shah 🌟 — a student who wanted to make campus life simpler, smarter, and more special for everyone. 🧠💙
  • Q: I have a suggestion or need help. What should I do?
    A: Just reach out via the Contact page or email us at support@campusconnect.in. We’d love to hear from you! 🤝📬
